WHO WE ARE



Afkanerd is an idea, the idea being "We code for the people". The ideology evolved from the beleive in human rights, freedom of speech and expression. We fight against capitalism and we do it all for the people. Our code base is all open source.

WHAT WE DO



We use modern technologies to build sustainable solutions that benefit the people. We primarily focus on open-source software for health care, education and communication. We build in the open and encourage you to join us on our mission to make the world better and more accessible.

WHY WE DO IT



We are inspired by many great individuals who take action. We know that we can only inspire change and drive development by leading the way so we are! We hope to be the change that we expect and are doing our part to get there.

HOW TO JOIN US



Every one is welcome to join in on our current and future open-source projects. Together we can improve and grow. We strongly advocate for open-source culture and truly beleive it is the best way to develop better software solutions. Take a look at ongoing projects to learn more.
